# Welcome!!

Our group makes research on out-of-equilibrium materials using theoretical physics. We would like to make robust theoretical tools with strong predictive power. Our aim is the deep understanding of non-equilibrium properties of soft matter using and developing theoretical tools. We combine fundamental research of non-equilibrium thermodynamics with molecular dynamics simulations in the study of real out-of-equilibrium soft materials. The research interests of the group are the following:

  1. Theoretical non-equilibruim thermodynamics.
  2. Structural and Dynamical properties of complex liquids.
  3. Ultra-slow behavior of glass-forming liquids.
  4. Ionic Liquids and its applications.
  5. Modeling of biomolecules in solution.
